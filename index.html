## Laboratory StoKaChic jewelry was born within the Laboratoire de Mathématiques d'Orsay, a research laboratory in fundamental mathematics affiliated with the CNRS and Université Paris-Saclay. Established during the development of the Saclay scientific hub in the second half of the 20th century, the laboratory has become a leading center for mathematical research in France and internationally. The laboratory is particularly renowned for its work in probability theory, analysis, geometry, and dynamical systems. Over time, the LMO has hosted and trained numerous internationally recognized mathematicians. Among them are Jean-Christophe Yoccoz, awarded the Fields Medal in 1994 for his work in dynamical systems, and Wendelin Werner, awarded the Fields Medal in 2006, whose research in probability theory has profoundly reshaped the understanding of random phenomena and interfaces in statistical physics.
